    Description

    Introducing AICat, the ultimate AI assistant powered by OpenAI. With lightning-fast response times, AICat is the perfect companion for all your daily tasks.

    AICat comes with local storage of chat history, so you can easily access your past conversations and pick up where you left off. You can also customize your prompts to get the most relevant responses for your needs.

    AICat supports Markdown display and code highlighting, making it easy to share and collaborate on code snippets and technical information. With support for custom GPT models such as GPT-3 and GPT-4, AICat can provide even more accurate and personalized responses.

    You can also adjust parameters such as context message count and temperature to fine-tune your AI experience.

    For macOS users, AICat offers quick access from the menu bar, so you can get the answers you need without interrupting your workflow.

    Download AICat today and experience the convenience and power of having an AI assistant at your fingertips.
